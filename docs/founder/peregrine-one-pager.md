# Peregrine One-Pager

## What Peregrine Is
Peregrine is the licensed execution home for the right opportunities surfaced through FALCO.

FALCO is the origination, screening, and review system.
Peregrine is where the right files can be worked through a compliant, execution-side channel.

## Why Peregrine Exists
FALCO and Peregrine do different jobs.

FALCO:
- finds distress early
- cleans up the file
- packages the strongest candidates
- routes them into controlled review

Peregrine:
- provides the licensed execution home
- gives the right opportunities a proper channel to move through
- creates a cleaner bridge from intelligence to action

## What Peregrine Is Not
Peregrine is not:
- a public marketing brand
- a replacement for FALCO
- a generic lead-selling operation
- meant to blur the difference between origination and execution

## Why The Separation Matters
Keeping FALCO and Peregrine distinct helps:
- preserve product clarity
- keep operator trust
- support compliance and execution structure
- avoid confusing intelligence with actual deal execution

## What Kind Of Home Peregrine Needs
The right home for Peregrine should offer:
- licensed credibility
- operational fit
- room to execute the right files
- alignment with a relationship-driven, partner-first model

The wrong home would be:
- unclear on role
- too broad or unfocused
- interested in the idea but not the workflow
- not serious about real execution

## What A Good Fit Looks Like
A good Peregrine home is one where:
- the role is clear
- there is real operator or broker alignment
- upstream files can be routed into an actual execution path
- the structure makes the system stronger, not more confusing

## How To Explain Peregrine In One Sentence
Use this:

Peregrine is the licensed execution side that gives the right FALCO-sourced opportunities a real home to move through.

## What Needs To Be Decided This Week
- Is Peregrine independent, aligned, or housed under another operator/broker?
- What exact role should it play?
- What does the host or partner gain?
- What makes the structure strong and credible from day one?

## Near-Term Goal
The goal this week is not to overbuild Peregrine.
The goal is to place it in the right structure so FALCO has a credible execution-side path when the right files appear.
